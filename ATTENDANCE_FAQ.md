# Attendance Scanning FAQ

## General Questions

### Q: How does the QR code scanning work?
**A:** Students show their QR codes (on phones, ID cards, or printed sheets). Teachers scan them using their device camera. The system automatically identifies the student and marks them present with visual and haptic feedback.

### Q: What if a student doesn't have a QR code?
**A:** Use the manual checkboxes to mark attendance. The system allows both scanning and manual methods.

### Q: Can I take attendance outside school hours?
**A:** The system validates attendance between 7:00 AM and 6:00 PM. Outside these hours, you'll see a warning but can still mark attendance manually (validation status is recorded).

## Technical Issues

### Q: The camera won't start. What should I do?
**A:** Check that:
- Camera permissions are granted in your browser
- You're using a supported browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)
- Your device has a working camera
- Try refreshing the page

### Q: I'm getting "Outside School Area" warning.
**A:** This means you're more than 500 meters from the school. Move closer to school premises, or use manual marking (the warning will be logged with the attendance record).

### Q: The QR code won't scan. What's wrong?
**A:** Try:
- Better lighting
- Holding device 6-12 inches from the QR code
- Keeping the QR code level with the camera
- Ensuring the QR code isn't damaged or dirty

### Q: The system shows I'm offline. Can I still take attendance?
**A:** Yes! Attendance saves locally and syncs automatically when you reconnect. You'll see a "pending sync" counter showing how many records need to be uploaded.

## Student-Related Questions

### Q: How do students get their QR codes?
**A:** QR codes are generated automatically when students are added to classes. They can access them through their student portal or the school can print them on ID cards.

### Q: What if a student forgets their QR code?
**A:** Teachers can manually mark the student present using the checkbox system.

### Q: Can students scan their own attendance?
**A:** No, the system is designed for teacher-controlled scanning to ensure accuracy and prevent cheating.

## Security & Privacy

### Q: Is location data stored?
**A:** Yes, GPS coordinates are recorded with attendance for audit purposes and to validate that attendance was taken at school.

### Q: Who can see attendance data?
**A:** Teachers can see their own class attendance. Administrators have access to all attendance records for reporting and auditing.

### Q: Is the data secure?
**A:** All data is encrypted and stored securely. The system includes validation checks to prevent unauthorized attendance marking.

## Advanced Features

### Q: What does the progress bar show?
**A:** It displays the percentage of students marked present out of the total class size (e.g., "15 of 25 students present (60%)").

### Q: Can I mark students as late?
**A:** Yes, use the dropdown menu next to each student to set their status as Present, Absent, or Late.

### Q: How do I refresh location/time validation?
**A:** Click the "🔄 Refresh Location & Time" button if the indicators seem incorrect.

### Q: What happens if I close the browser before submitting?
**A:** Attendance data is saved locally. When you reopen the page, your previous entries will be restored.
