# Training Video Script: How to Use Attendance Scanning

## Video Title: "Mastering Attendance Scanning in 5 Minutes"

## Introduction (0:00 - 0:30)
**Narrator:** "Welcome to our quick guide on using the school's new QR code attendance system. Whether you're a new teacher or just need a refresher, this video will show you everything you need to know to take attendance efficiently and accurately."

## Section 1: Getting Started (0:30 - 1:30)
**Narrator:** "First, log in to your teacher account and navigate to your class dashboard. Click on the class you want to take attendance for."

**[Screen shows login process and dashboard navigation]**

**Narrator:** "Before you begin, check these three important status indicators at the top of the page:
- Green 'Online' indicator shows you're connected
- 'Within School Area' confirms you're in the right location
- 'Valid Time' ensures it's between 7 AM and 6 PM"

**[Screen highlights each indicator with callouts]**

**Narrator:** "If any of these show warnings, you may still take attendance manually, but the system will log the validation issues."

## Section 2: Manual Attendance (1:30 - 2:30)
**Narrator:** "You can mark attendance manually using the checkboxes and dropdowns. Simply check the box next to a student's name to mark them present, or use the dropdown to set them as absent or late."

**[Screen demonstrates clicking checkboxes and using dropdowns]**

**Narrator:** "The progress bar at the top shows you how many students are marked present out of the total class size. Color-coded cards make it easy to see at a glance:
- Green cards for present students
- Yellow for late arrivals
- Gray for absent students"

## Section 3: QR Code Scanning (2:30 - 4:00)
**Narrator:** "For faster attendance taking, use the QR code scanner. Click the 'Start QR Scan' button and allow camera access when prompted."

**[Screen shows button click and permission prompt]**

**Narrator:** "Point your device's camera at the student's QR code. Hold steady about 6-12 inches away. The system will automatically:
1. Read the QR code
2. Identify the student
3. Mark them as present
4. Give you visual feedback with a green flash
5. Provide haptic feedback with device vibration"

**[Screen shows scanning process with effects]**

**Narrator:** "If there's an error, you'll see a red flash and feel triple vibrations. Common issues include poor lighting, damaged QR codes, or the student not being in the class list."

## Section 4: Offline and Sync Features (4:00 - 4:45)
**Narrator:** "The system works even when you're offline. Attendance data saves locally and shows a 'pending sync' counter. When you reconnect to the internet, data syncs automatically in the background."

**[Screen shows offline indicators and sync process]**

## Section 5: Submitting Attendance (4:45 - 5:15)
**Narrator:** "When you're finished, review all the records and click 'Submit Attendance'. The system includes location data and validation status with each record for complete audit trails."

**[Screen shows submit process]**

## Conclusion (5:15 - 5:30)
**Narrator:** "That's it! With this system, taking attendance is faster, more accurate, and provides better security than traditional methods. Remember to always check your validation status before starting, and don't hesitate to use manual marking as a backup."

**[Screen shows final dashboard with completed attendance]**

**Narrator:** "For more detailed instructions, check the full guide in your school portal. Happy teaching!"

## Production Notes:
- **Video Length**: 5-6 minutes
- **Visual Style**: Clean, professional with callouts and highlights
- **Demo Device**: Use actual mobile device for realistic scanning demo
- **Audio**: Clear, friendly narration with background music
